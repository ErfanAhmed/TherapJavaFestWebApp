About Therap

Therap BD subsidiary of Therap Services, LLC. is a web-based service organization that provides an integrated solution for documentation, reporting and communication needs of agencies providing support to people with developmental disabilities. Therap BD has an excellent team with an expertise in Java. Therap's State of Art Technology, Integrated Suite of Online Applications and Excellent Global Team have collectively made Therap the market leader in the Developmental Disablility Industry in US.
