Welcome to Therap Java Fest

Welcome to Therap's first online Java Festival for university students all over the country! We thank you for your interest and wish you the very best for the contest!

This event is specifically designed to bring out the best in you and the best among you! During the course of the festival, you will get several opportunities to gather knowledge and apply it in real-world scenarios evolving as you create Java programs. You will also get multiple opportunities and platforms designed for you to discuss things you know or have learned.

We expect you to enthusiastically participate in all stages of the festival and give your 100% to make this endeavor a success.

Best of luck!
