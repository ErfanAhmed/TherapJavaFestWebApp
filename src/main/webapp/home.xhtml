Welcome to Therap Java Fest

The festival will start from 1st September, 2012. A dedicated Facebook page will serve as the entry point of the festival. A web application will be launched to facilitate the festival activities. A forum will be used to provide feedback to the participants. Interested participants will at first register individually and take a preliminary screening test designed to evaluate their basic programming skills. The selected participants will then form a group of two members for developing a java web application or android project. Each group will initially submit a project proposal and then will implement the project idea. After evaluating the projects, top ranked participants will be invited for a live presentation of their projects. Prizes will be awarded to the top three projects and the festival will be concluded with the prize giving ceremony on 3rd November, 2012.

Prizes:

  • First prize : two Google Nexus 7 16GB( 267 USD each including tax)
  • Second prize : two Google Nexus 7 8GB( 213 USD each including tax)
  • Third prize : two Amazon Kindle touch ( 107 USD each including tax)
